FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What fitness level is required for the tours?

Our tours range from Easy to Challenging. Each destination page lists the difficulty level. Most tours involve moderate hiking (3–6 hours/day) on established trails. We recommend a basic fitness level, but no professional training is needed. Our guides adapt the pace to your group.

What should I pack for a forest tour?

We send a detailed packing list after booking. Generally: sturdy hiking boots, moisture-wicking layers, rain jacket, hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, reusable water bottle, and a good camera. We provide all specialized equipment on-site.

Can I customize my tour itinerary?

Absolutely! Our Custom Itinerary service lets you tailor every aspect of your trip. Contact us with your preferences — preferred activities, pace, dietary needs, accommodation style — and our travel designers will craft a personalized adventure.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before your tour start date for a full refund. Cancellations within 3–7 days receive a 50% refund or full credit for a future tour. Within 3 days, no refund is available, but you may transfer your booking to another person.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included in the tour price, but we strongly recommend it. We can connect you with our partner insurance providers who offer comprehensive coverage for adventure travel at competitive rates.
